Overview

Utilizing an electronic smart filter and our community based app, our design allows smokers to plan and track their cessation as well as access to an online community for motivation and information.

  • Data
  • Health
  • Community

Nicotine & tar tracking

Monitor your progress throughout the week without having to manually catalog every smoke.

Social Community

Interact with other users that are quitting cigarettes to increase motivation.

Personalized Routine

After the initial set up process, Cease will generate a weekly plan to follow.

Detailed Information

Information from the smart cigarette filter is readily accessible and updated for each cigarette smoked.

Online Forum

Smart Filter
