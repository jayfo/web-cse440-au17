Features

Quickly add the various tasks you need to tackle, press one button,
and let StudyBuddy help you stay focused.

Get started quickly

Activate the sensors with the click of a button. Choose to track general study time or track time by assignment.

  • Track time generally or by task
  • Add new tasks as they come up
  • Pause and resume tasks

Sensors work with you

StudyBuddy connects all of your devices for the most accurate report of your time. Your webcam tracks your eye movements, while your devices track your usage.

  • Choose which sensors you use
  • Sensors only activate when you want
  • Helps you see how you spend time

Achieve Balance

StudyBuddy promotes balanced study habits.
You will be notified when it is time for a productive break, as well as when it is time to return to work.

  • Boost productivity
  • Option to postpone the break
  • Minimize long distractions
