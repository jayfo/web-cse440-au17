Features

Tools to keep you on track.

Unpack how you feel

Talking with the rapid AI allows you to get anything off of your mind you need to make you feel better. The feedback you give is used to generate schedule reminders and suggestions.

Schedule reminders

The RAPID app has the capabilities to link to your university's canvas as well as a personal calender to give you reminders on due dates. RAPID also uses the feedback you give to present schedule suggestions to you like that 6PM run that gives you a brain blast.

Analyze your feedback

Discover behavioral and emotional patterns you might not have recognized with RAPID's analytics, driven by your feedback.
