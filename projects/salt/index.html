Problem and Solution Overview

Every day small restaurants cater to a different number of customers with varying tastes. This often leads to an imbalance of ingredients making some ingredients go overstock while the others out of stock. This indirectly impacts food waste too. It is also important that these fluctuations be communicated properly between the servers, chef, and manager.

SALT – The Sales and Food Log Tracker, allows restaurants to track the food ordered by customers and the food ordered by the restaurant. SALT further helps our customers reflect on food waste
and gain actionable insights.

Process

Components of our design research process.

  • Research

    We conducted 4 semi-structured interviews to identify pain-points.

  • Ideate

    We used sketches and storyboards as tools to create and share ideas.

  • Prototype

    We created paper prototype and iterated on them to create digital mockups based on the
    evaluation feedback.

  • Evaluate

    We conducted heuristic evaluations and usability tests to evaluate our paper prototype.

How it works

Menu Status

View the status of the menu to determine what to upsell and the dishes that are out of stock.

Ingredient Stock

View the status of the ingredients to see the ingredients that are in stock and out of stock. Based on the stock of ingredients, SALT suggests dishes to prepare as specials.

Food Waste

View the food waste and the ingredients that contribute to it.
